About Bots

ITHB

About ITHB

ITHB Helps remind students to take brain breaks! Breaks are an essential part of learning. They help students decrease stress, increase productivity, and boost brain function.

Tip: We recommend you use HOB with ITHB. HOB will help organize your homework and ITHB will help you get through your homework.

HOB

About HOB

HOB is a simple robot that helps students organize their homework into "blocks". Sort of like a to do list. By doing this it'll make your homework assignments less scary. Once you create your homework blocks head over to ITHB!

ITHB will help you get through your homework blocks.

SLAB

About SLAB

SLAB aims to help make school, longer homework assignments, and homework, in general, be less stressful. The advice and resource section is a place for students to share advice, tips, or resources that they wish they would have known when they ran into some homework or school trouble.

If a resource or a piece of advice is popular or helpful it could potentially become a Study Bot or a part of a Study Bot! If you have some advice, tips, or a resource that you want to be posted on our Resource & Advice page, we'd be more than happy to help share it!

Email us at studybots1@gmail.com or fill out the form below. Your real name will be kept anonymous unless you'd like us to use a username you make up.

WWC

About WWC

WWC makes school clubs "global", so students can join, see, and partner up with them. If you don't like any of the clubs available you can either purpose your own club and be the president of it or you can make a blank club and just be a member or other role in the club. Join WWC today!
