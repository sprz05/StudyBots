Questions & Answers:

What are Bots?

Bots take a study habit or skill and make them more interactive. So you can utilize the benefits of healthy and effective habits easily, and enjoyably.

What does BETA mean?

You might notice the word Beta next to some Bots names. This just means this bot is new and would appreciate any feedback or ideas you have for them!
